Why this proves Verity works on any transaction
Verity does not pattern-match specific transactions. It checks mathematically defined fields in the Algorand protocol specification. A rekey field in ANY transaction from ANY dApp — whether built by us or not — will always be caught. The detection is cryptographically deterministic.
